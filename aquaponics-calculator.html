Compatibility is temperature-based: the fish's comfort midpoint sets the shared-loop temperature, and each crop is rated by whether that falls in its preferred (good), tolerable (workable) or outside (poor) root-zone range. "Poor" pairs aren't impossible — they imply a decoupled design with separate water loops. Monthly cash-flow model: year 0 = investment; fish income starts after the grow-out lag (continuous stocking), crops after the first cycle, costs from month 1 (full feed, slightly conservative). Break-even = first month cumulative turns positive. Excluded: financing/interest, tax, working capital, permits/effluent/hygiene. High-value fish/crustaceans (pikeperch, prawn, noble crayfish) are indicative niche species. Defaults: BDEW/Bundesnetzagentur grid prices, Fraunhofer ISE PV cost, EEG 2026 feed-in, Amt für Statistik Berlin-Brandenburg land lease, regional commercial rents, German direct-marketing produce & farm-gate prices, statutory minimum wage 2026.
